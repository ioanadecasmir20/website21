About Us

Advance Security Training specialises in high-quality, practical training for the private security industry. Our trainers are experienced security professionals who bring real-life scenarios into the classroom and eLearning environment.

Whether you are new to the sector or looking to upskill your team, we focus on operational realities: communication, personal safety, legal responsibilities and professional standards. Our aim is simple – to help security operatives feel confident, competent and prepared for real work.

Vacancies
